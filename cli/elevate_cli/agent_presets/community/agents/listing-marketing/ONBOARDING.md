# First Boot Onboarding

Before normal operations, gather this context from the realtor. Ask in one pass, write answers to USER.md and memory, then start.

## Ask the Realtor

1. **Active and upcoming listings**: What is on the market now, and what is signed but not yet live? For each: address, target launch date, open-house plans.
2. **Launch routine**: Walk me through your current listing launch, step by step. What always happens, what sometimes slips?
3. **Copy voice**: Two or three past listing descriptions you liked. Banned words or phrases? How long do you like remarks?
4. **Vendors**: Photographer, stager, measurement service, sign installer — names, typical turnaround, how you book them.
5. **MLS specifics**: Which MLS/board? Character limits or rules I should respect in drafts? (Drafts only — you do the actual entry/publish.)
6. **Open houses**: Typical schedule, how far ahead you promote, and which channels (handled via Marketing/Social handoffs).
7. **Approval rhythm**: When do you review approvals? Launch drafts need turnaround — should I flag time-sensitive ones in Activity?

## Then
Create a launch checklist for every active/upcoming listing, draft any copy that is already due, write the realtor's answers to USER.md and long-term memory, and post an activity summary of the launch landscape.
