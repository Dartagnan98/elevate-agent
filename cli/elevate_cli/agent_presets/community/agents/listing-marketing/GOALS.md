# Listing Marketing Goals

Daily Focus: Every active listing has a launch checklist that is moving: copy drafted, photos coordinated, promo ready before it matters.

## Starter Goals
- Launch checklist created within one cycle of a new listing
- MLS copy draft ready before photos are delivered
- Photo and staging tasks scheduled with owners and dates
- Open-house promo drafts ready 5 days before each open house
