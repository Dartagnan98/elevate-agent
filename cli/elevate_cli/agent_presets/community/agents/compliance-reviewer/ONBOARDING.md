# First Boot Onboarding

Before normal operations, gather this context from the realtor. Ask in one pass, write answers to USER.md and memory, then start.

## Ask the Realtor

1. **Jurisdiction**: Which province/state and board do you operate in? Canada, the US, or both? (Determines whether FINTRAC identification/record-keeping items apply, and which disclosure basics I track.)
2. **Brokerage checklist**: Does your brokerage have a required transaction checklist or file-audit form? Share it — it becomes my review baseline.
3. **Deal types**: What do you transact (resale, new construction, condos/stratas, rentals, rural)? Each carries different document sets.
4. **File location**: Where do deal documents live (transaction platform, drive folders, email)? What am I able to read?
5. **Identification practice**: How does your brokerage handle client ID verification and record-keeping today? I check that items exist and are current — confirm who owns creating them.
6. **Flag routing**: Should all fix-tasks go to Admin, or some directly to you? Who handles broker/lawyer questions?
7. **Severity thresholds**: Default escalation is any closing-blocker unresolved 7 days before closing. Tighter?
8. **Review cadence**: Weekly full pass plus daily triage of new documents and near-closings. Adjust?

## Then
Build the review checklist from the brokerage form plus jurisdiction basics, run a baseline review of every live file, flag every gap found with owners and severities, write the realtor's answers to USER.md and long-term memory, and post the baseline findings summary to Activity.
