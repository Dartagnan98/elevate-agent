# Elevate 1.2.42 — release notes

The steer bar is back.

## Choose how a mid-run message lands

While the agent is working, a bar above the message box now offers the
choice: **Steer mid-run** injects your message into the running turn (it
cuts in mid-think, marked in the timeline where it landed), **Queue for
after** holds it until the current turn finishes. Your pick applies to the
sends you make during that turn and resets to your default when the turn
ends.
