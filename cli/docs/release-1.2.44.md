# Elevate 1.2.44 — release notes

Steering now reads like a conversation.

## Enter queues; the bar steers

Pressing Enter while the agent is working always places your message in the
queue — it appears as a bar above the message box with a **Steer now** button
on the right to inject it into the running turn, and **Cancel** to drop it.

## Your steer gets answered in place

When a steer is injected, the chat now splits at that exact moment: the work
done so far freezes into its card, ending with a "Conversation steered" marker
that shows when you cut in — and the run continues in a fresh section BELOW
your message, where the answer lands. No more steered messages dangling under
a streaming response looking ignored.

## The steer bar matches the composer theme

The strip renders as a proper bar — same card styling as the workspace bar —
instead of floating text.
